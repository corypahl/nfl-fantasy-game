NFL Fantasy Team Builder

How to Play:

Build your NFL fantasy lineup! A random team will be shown. Select a position (QB, RB, WR1, WR2, TE, DEF) for that team. You won't know which team is next. Your score is based on the selected players' previous season fantasy points. Aim for the highest score and the best grade!
